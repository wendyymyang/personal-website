ABOUT ME

Clean, semantic code? I'm a fan. Turning beautiful designs into beautiful websites? I'm a fan. You, who's checking out my humble webpage? I'm a fan!

Hi, I'm Wendy, a newly-minted front-end developer. Ever since being introduced to it, I've become obsessed with coding. I love challenging myself to build websites that are accessible, clean and responsive, and I look forward to building up my arsenal of front-end development tools.

If I'm not coding away with a deadly concentration, I can be found reading Haruki Murakami novels, making myself a smoothie, attending fitness bootcamps, and openly admiring other people's dogs. In addition to these, I also like to tweet, take photos on my trusty Canon 6D, answer questions I may or may not be qualified to answer, and write blog entries.
